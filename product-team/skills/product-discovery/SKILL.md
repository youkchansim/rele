# Product Discovery

Continuous Discovery framework based on Teresa Torres' methodology. Structure the path from outcomes to validated solutions.

## Discovery Workflow

1. **Define Desired Outcome** — Business metric tied to product strategy
2. **Discover Opportunities** — User needs, pain points, desires (via research)
3. **Map Solutions** — Generate multiple solutions per opportunity
4. **Test Assumptions** — Identify and validate riskiest assumptions first

## Opportunity Solution Tree (OST)

Visual framework: Outcome -> Opportunities -> Solutions -> Experiments.
