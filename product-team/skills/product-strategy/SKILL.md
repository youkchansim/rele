# Product Strategy

Strategic planning frameworks for product direction, business model, and value proposition.

## Strategy Workflow

1. **Assess Current State** — Metrics, market position, competitive landscape
2. **Define Vision & Direction** — Long-term product vision, strategic bets
3. **Structure the Model** — Business model, value proposition, growth engine
4. **Set Guardrails** — Trade-offs, what we won't do, success criteria
