# Market Analysis

Strategic market analysis frameworks for understanding competitive landscape and market opportunity.

## Framework Selection Guide

| Situation | Recommended Framework | Why |
|-----------|----------------------|-----|
| Internal + external assessment | SWOT | Balanced strengths/weaknesses/opportunities/threats view |
| Macro-environment scanning | PESTLE | Covers political, economic, social, tech, legal, environmental |
| Industry competition analysis | Porter's 5 Forces | Structural competitive pressure analysis |
| Market opportunity sizing | TAM/SAM/SOM | Quantifies addressable market at each level |

## SWOT + PESTLE
